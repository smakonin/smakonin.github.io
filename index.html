Biography

I am a Postdoctoral Fellow in Engineering Science at Simon Fraser University working with Ivan V. Bajic. Previously, I worked as a Postdoctoral Fellow with Wolfgang Stuerzlinger for one year in Computing Science. I received my PhD in Computing Science at Simon Fraser University in 2014 under the supervision of Fred Popowich. I graduated with my BTech from the British Columbia Institute of Technology in 2009. Before finishing my Bachelor's, I worked in industry as a software architect and software developer for over 12 years and as an applied researcher for 2 years.

Currently, I am the Chair of the IEEE Vancouver Joint Computing Chapter (incl. Computational Intelligence). I am a Senior Member of the IEEE and a member of the ACM and AAAI. I am also a registered Information Systems Professional with the Canadian Information Processing Society.

My research interests include data science, computational sustainability, disaggregation (non-intrusive load monitoring or NILM), and software engineering. Other interests include open science; the making of hardware and software open source and the creation of public datasets.
